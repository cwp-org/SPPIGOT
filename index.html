Welcome

Use the SPPIGOT to see how your municipal, Tribal, or territorial government operations address stormwater pollution prevention (P2) and identify actions for integrating P2 in your community.

The SPPIGOT was created in 2026 by the Center for Watershed Protection as part of a toolkit called Integrating Stormwater Pollution Prevention into Municipal, Tribal, and Territorial Government Operations: A Toolkit for EPA Region 2 Communities. The Center for Watershed Protection received financial support from EPA under an Assistance Agreement to create this toolkit.

How it works

  1. Screening. For six common government operations, indicate whether your government has control and how important the operation is as a pollution source. The tool ranks each as high or low priority for pollution prevention.
  2. P2 Assessment. For each high-priority operation, answer a short set of yes/no questions about current practices. You can skip any question.
  3. Results & Actions. The tool generates a tailored list of recommended pollution prevention actions, which you can filter by pollutants addressed and co-benefits and print to PDF.
